What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and login status.

How We Use Cookies

  • To remember your login information and keep you signed in
  • To store items in your shopping cart while you browse
  • To analyze website traffic and improve user experience
  • To show relevant product recommendations and promotional offers
  • To collect anonymized usage data to help optimize our site

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for core site functionality, such as security, shopping cart access, and account login.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website so we can improve performance and usability.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as language, region, and display settings.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads based on your browsing history or interests. These cookies may come from our ad partners.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party services like Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and other marketing tools that may place cookies on your device. These cookies are controlled by the respective third parties and governed by their privacy policies.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control or block cookies through your settings. You can delete existing cookies, prevent them from being set, or configure your browser to alert you before accepting a cookie. However, please note that disabling essential cookies may impact the functionality of our site.

To manage your cookie settings, refer to the instructions provided by your browser:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions
