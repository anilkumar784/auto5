Privacy Policy

At OEMFitParts, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you visit our website, create an account, place an order, or contact our support team, we may collect information including your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and payment details. We may also collect browser data and usage information through cookies to improve our website and services.

How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfill orders and provide requested services
  • To send order confirmations, tracking information, and updates
  • To offer customer support and respond to inquiries
  • To personalize your shopping experience
  • To send optional promotional emails and updates (with your consent)

Data Security

We take your data security seriously. All transactions on our site are encrypted using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) technology. Additionally, we use secure third-party payment processors that are PCI DSS compliant to ensure that your credit card information is protected. Access to your personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Third-Party Services

We partner with select third-party providers for shipping, payment processing, and email marketing. These providers are only given access to the information necessary to perform their services and are not permitted to use your data for other purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. You may request deletion of your account data at any time by contacting our support team.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal data. You may also withdraw consent or object to certain uses of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@oemfitparts.com.

Compliance

OEMFitParts complies with all applicable privacy laws and regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Union, and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for residents of California. If you believe your data has been mishandled, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

This policy may be updated from time to time. We encourage you to review it periodically for any changes. Your continued use of our site constitutes your acceptance of our privacy practices.
